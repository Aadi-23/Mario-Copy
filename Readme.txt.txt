This project is a remake of Mario. Open the sln file, and press f5 to play. To exit, press Escape.

Controls:

A/Left Key to move Left
D/Right Key to move Right
Space to Jump

Jump over enemies to kill them. If they touch you from left or right, you lose health. 3 health loss is game over. If you fall through the ground, you lose the game. To win the game, you need to reach the flag at the end of the level.
